About US

Our activities
Networking for Impact

We specialize in organizing influential networking and recruiting events. By bringing together diverse stakeholders with the skills and knowledge needed in our interconnected world to innovate solutions for complex challenges posed by digital advancements, economic crises, and environmental issues.

Community building

We build and advise peer support organizations and networks which form bridges between Finnish and international people. We provide consultation and hold advisory roles in many of these organizations to help them build healthy sustainable communities with the values of global citizenship.

Professional development

We provide workshops, speaking events and research to help your organization, school or business learn how to add value and sustainability to your practices. Through global citizenship skills we promote the knowledge and values that build happy, healthy, sustainable working communities, practices and environments.

Social and Cultural events

We organize and host several different types of social and cultural events with the aim of promoting positive exchanges and solidarity in society. These fun events allow guests to experience all the joy and deep understanding of the possibilities intercultural exchanges can bring.

About our foundation

Uniting Finnish and International people in solidarity for a better future!

The world is rapidly changing due to digital technology, international travel and migration, economic crises, conflicts, and environmental degradation. Our NGO understands the worldwide need to adapt how we work, teach and learn, to solve complex challenges.

Our mission is shaping more aware, harmonious, skilled and sustainable communities through engaging Global Citizenship Education activities. We specialize in organizing impactful networking events, offering expert consulting services, organizing community projects and delivering educational programs which cultivate global awareness. Unite is committed to equipping individuals, communities, organizations and businesses with the tools necessary to meet the challenges to our increasingly interconnected world.
