Our projects

Market of possibilities (Mahdollisuuksien Tori),Lahti

In partnership with Fingo - Finnish Development NGOs network we organize and operate the Market of possibilities (Mahdollisuuksien Tori) project in Lahti, Finland. The project is a smaller scale iteration of Helsinki's World Village Festival which is free of charge to the public and allows over 50,000 visitors to learn about global development NGOs in Finland while experiencing a fun international festival.
