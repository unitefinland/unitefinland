Our Board

Irisa Anne Keahey

Founder and President at UNITE- Maailmankansalaisia yhdessä ry,
University of Oulu Intercultural education MA

Irisa is passionately pursuing a sustainable and peaceful future through community building. She focuses on the human side of the SDGs by developing solidarity and community partnerships for complex challenges. Her background is in education and outreach projects with diverse communities. Her research interests are intercultural skills, global citizenship skills, thicker democracy, positive peace, technological impact on globalizing society and decolonial education. She is currently researching transitional justice at the University of Oulu.

Anna-Leena Atkinson

Co-founder and Advisor at UNITE- Maailmankansalaisia yhdessä ry,
Nationwide Service Director at Arffman

Anna-Leena is passionate about equality, diverse work environments and open dialogues which bring multiple perspectives. She is an immigration specialist working as the nationwide head of virtual teams for Arffman Oy, a company providing online integration training in Finnish for The Finnish Ministry of Economic Affairs and Employment. She has been working on integration and inclusion issues for over 20 years as part of national programs. She believes encounters are my never-ending source of inspiration!.

Josefina Pönkkä

Co-founder and Advisor at UNITE- Maailmankansalaisia yhdessä ry,
Senior Marketing and Communications Specialist at Wipak

Josefina is a sustainable future maker who deeply cares about international issues. She advocates for internationalization and intercultural exchange as an international marketing manager and international travel influencer. Her work emphasizes the beauty of Finnish culture and international intercultural dialogues. She supports Wipak Group's global team by developing internal and external communications strategies, creating and managing engaging content that highlights the company's work as a leader in sustainable packaging around the world.
