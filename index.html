Uniting Finnish and International people in solidarity for a better future!

Our mission is shaping more aware, harmonious, skilled and sustainable communities through engaging Global Citizenship Education activities. We specialize in organizing impactful networking events, offering expert consulting services, organizing community projects and delivering educational programs which cultivate global awareness. Unite is committed to equipping individuals, communities, organizations and businesses with the tools necessary to meet the challenges to our increasingly interconnected world.

Our activities
Global Citizenship Education

Engage communities through impactful educational programs fostering global awareness, equipping individuals with the skills and knowledge needed in our interconnected world.

Networking for Impact

Specialize in organizing influential networking events, bringing together diverse stakeholders to collaborate on solutions for complex challenges posed by digital advancements, economic crises, and environmental issues.

Consulting for Change

Provide expert consulting services to empower communities, organizations, and businesses, offering strategic guidance to adapt and thrive amidst the dynamic landscape shaped by international travel, migration, conflicts, and technological shifts.
